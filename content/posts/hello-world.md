This site just got wiped to the studs — again — and rebuilt on [Jaspr](https://jaspr.site), a Dart web framework by Kilian Schulte.

## Why Jaspr?

Short version: I know Dart. The Astro rebuild worked, but I was rubber-stamping Claude's output because I couldn't meaningfully read half of it. On a Dart stack I can push back.

## What's here

Right now: a home page and this post, wired through `jaspr_content` with the stock `BlogLayout`. Everything else — styling, navigation, better post listing — is TBD.
