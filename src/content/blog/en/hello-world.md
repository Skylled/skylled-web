Every site needs a first post. This is mine.

I've rebuilt this site from scratch using [Astro](https://astro.build) after the previous Flutter web version started feeling like more maintenance than it was worth. The goal was something simple: a place to put my thoughts, and a page that tells you who I am.

## What to expect here

I'll write about things I find interesting — mostly Android, open source, and whatever dev rabbit holes I fall into. No schedule, no promise of consistency. Just posts when I have something worth saying.

If you want to reach me, the links on the [About page](/about) are the best way.
